2023 Met Gala


On 12/16/2023 Concrete Rose 773 Hosted their first Met Gala. The goal of the gala was to bring joy to the hearts of Chicago's youth by collecting toy donations that will make this holiday season truly special. We had over 170 guest and recieved hundreds of toys. We were thankful to partner with BrightPoint who will ensure the gifts are donated to children just before Christmas Day. We would like to thank each of our sponsors for their commitment to the event and their role in accomplishing the mission. To everyone that liked, commented, shared, reposted, donated, and committed their time to help, we truly appreciate you. We are looking forward to next year already! Thank you all for a tremendous event!
