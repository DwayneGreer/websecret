Our Community

Concrete Rose has a multitude of individuals in its community. We host kids from elementary schools all over the south side of chicago and they are mentored by teens and adults who have either attended a highschool in Chicago or are currently attending one. Concrete Rose is always looking to expand its community, so always feel free to reach out.
